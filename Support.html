Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if I can't log into my account?

Make sure you are using the correct email and password. If you forgot your password, use the "Forgot Password" feature to reset it.

How can I request a refund?

To request a refund, go to the purchases section in your account settings and select the game you wish to refund. Follow the prompts to complete your request.
